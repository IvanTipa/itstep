Delicious Pasta Carbonara Recipe

Pasta Carbonara is a classic Italian dish that combines the richness of eggs and cheese with the savory goodness of pancetta or bacon. Here's a simple recipe to recreate this mouthwatering dish at home.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Pancetta or bacon
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta al dente.
  2. In a separate pan, cook the pancetta or bacon until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper in a bowl.
  4. Combine the cooked pasta with the egg mixture, adding the pancetta. Toss until well-coated.
  5. Serve immediately, garnished with additional Parmesan and black pepper.
